Our results

  • Finalist Alliance

    During the Open Robotics Intelligent Grid summer camp in Timișoara, organized by the West University of Timișoara in collaboration with the CSH robotics team, we reached the finals, placing 2nd overall.

    September 2024

  • Motivate Award III

    In the 2023-2024 season of the international robotics competition FIRST Tech Challenge, at the regional stage in Bucharest, we won the Motivate Award, 3rd place. This award highlights our team’s strong involvement in the competition community through the promotion of FIRST values, such as Gracious Professionalism.

    January 2024

  • Winning Alliance

    During the Open Robotics Intelligent Grid summer camp in Timișoara, organized by the West University of Timișoara in collaboration with the CSH robotics team, we were part of the winning alliance, returning home with the grand prize.

    September 2023

  • Design Award III

    In the 2021-2022 season of the international robotics competition FIRST Tech Challenge, our team, under the name SovaTech, received the Design Award, 3rd place, at the regional stage in Bucharest. The award recognized the functional and aesthetic integration of industrial design elements in our robot’s appearance.

    January 2022

  • Think Award II

    In the 2020-2021 season of the international robotics competition FIRST Tech Challenge, our club, under the name SovaTech, won the Think Award, 2nd place, at the regional stage in Bucharest. The award was granted for our engineering notebook, which presented the robot and game strategy in an exemplary manner.

    January 2021
