About us

Zenith, the robotics team of "Grigore Moisil" National College, is more than just a club for young tech enthusiasts. It is a community, dedicated to innovation and creativity in one of the most fascinating areas of our century: robotics. United by this common passion, we work every day to reach the highest level, participating in various national and international competitions. For us, Zenith has become a space of personal and professional development, where each member brings their unique contribution, contributing to collective success. Every challenge is an opportunity to expand our knowledge and innovate, and mutual support and collaboration help us overcome any obstacle and evolve.

Promoting STEAM and technical thinking among students.

Rebranding

Our story begins in 2020, when a group of young people from the 10th and 11th grades of the "Grigore Moisil" National College set up the SovaTech team with the help of our coordinating teacher Mihaela Garabet. As the years passed, the members who brought the initiative to develop in the STEAM field became mentors, leaving behind a new generation to perpetuate the work of the club. The new members, learning from the mistakes of the previous series, moved on, bringing a new vision to the club and thus set out to reinvent itself under the name Zenith.

Our values

Collaboration

The collaborative working environment allows us to freely exchange ideas, sharing knowledge and developing friendships. The jobs of the future will be based on teamwork, and the club our promote this skill.

Innovation

Currently, we aim to shape the next generation of engineers. We wish to showcase the power and importance of innovative and creative thinking, backed by science.

Gracious professionalism

Gracious proffesionalism is based on encouraging competition based on respect and fairness where the main goal is not gain, but mutual help. Thus, performance becomes a pretext for the personal and professional development of the participants.

Responsibility

This value is the basis of planning and organizing our activities, whilst setting objectives in the short or long term, thus achieving our desired result. This quality is essential for our club, as it may help us develop a good mindset for later on through adulthood.

Perseverance

We wish to progress and improve our activity, learning something useful every day. Only through perseverance will we get to our desired performance, which is why we consider this trait one of our defining values.
